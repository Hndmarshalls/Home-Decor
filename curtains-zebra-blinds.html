Zebra Blinds

Sleek and modern window solutions for the contemporary home.

Zebra Blinds, also known as Day and Night blinds, offer the ultimate in versatile light management. By alternating sheer and solid fabric stripes, you can easily shift between complete privacy and filtered natural light. This innovative design adds a contemporary, dynamic look to your windows, providing both the softness of a curtain and the functional control of a blind.
