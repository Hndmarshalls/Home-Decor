About Us

Luxury Redefined

HOME&DECOR is a trusted home décor and furnishing company with over 10 years of experience in delivering premium interior solutions for residential and commercial spaces. We specialize in wallpapers, curtains, blinds, upholstery, wall panels, flooring, rugs, and home décor accessories, offering a perfect balance of style, comfort, and durability.

With more than a decade of expertise in the interior furnishing and décor industry, HOME&DECOR has successfully served homeowners, architects, interior designers, hotels, offices, and retail spaces. Our deep understanding of materials, design trends, and installation techniques allows us to deliver interiors that are both elegant and long-lasting.

We provide customized furnishing solutions, including made-to-measure curtains, digital printed wallpapers, bespoke upholstery, decorative wall panels, and advanced flooring systems. From initial consultation and product selection to professional installation, our experienced team ensures a seamless and hassle-free experience.

At HOME&DECOR, we source our products from reputed brands and manufacturers, ensuring high quality, superior finish, and reliable performance. Whether it’s a single feature wall, a complete home makeover, or a large commercial project, we bring expertise, creativity, and precision to every space.

With 10+ years of industry experience, a customer-first approach, and a passion for design excellence, HOME&DECOR continues to be a preferred destination for complete interior décor solutions under one roof.
